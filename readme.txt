饥荒分组生存对抗 - 猪王争霸
你们要的饥荒分组生存对抗来了，玩腻了饥荒无止境的生存，不妨来试试这个MOD吧。
简介：该MOD允许你将玩家最多分为4个阵营，每个阵营都有一个猪王，你需要和你的队友保护你们的猪王，通过生存或击杀敌人得分赢取比赛胜利。

重要提示：
1.需要在无尽-PVP模式下开启该MOD，游戏中按Ctrl+F攻击敌对玩家和成员，可设置和平天数。
2.按Shift键冲刺，按Y键组内聊天，按U键对所有人聊天。
3.尽量不要和地穴一起使用，不保证兼容地下世界。如果游戏崩溃请关闭其他MOD，可能与其他MOD冲突。

--------------------------- 内容说明 ----------------------------

胜利条件
获取到指定的分数
杀掉其他阵营的猪王，只剩自己的猪王
规则很简单，两个条件任意达成其一就能取得最后的胜利！

提示
共4个阵营供玩家选择，MOD设置页面可设置阵营数量，PVP模式下同阵营玩家不能相互攻击
将一些有价值的物品给自己的猪王可以换取分数
击杀其他阵营玩家和部分怪物可以换取分数
猪王附近的建筑和农作物会被保护
自己阵营的猪王被杀时阵营会被解散，财产将被击杀势力占有(也可设置猪王不可杀)
想要赢得比赛，前提是要保护好自己的猪王，发展和武力并存，猪人永远是你最好的帮手
可指定选择队伍，也可随机队伍，可在MOD设置里进行设置(长期服务器建议随机队伍，开黑玩则指定队伍)
前期将会有一段和平发展期，不能攻击敌方成员(具体时间可在MOD设置中修改)

建议在PVP模式下开启该MOD，也可把猪王设置成不可杀，单纯地竞技得分，如果有想法和建议的可以留言，谢谢

--------------------------- English ----------------------------

DST Team War - PigKingCraft
Group survival and PvP, survival and protect your pig king, 4 teams at most.
If you are tired of playing the Old DST, try this mod and try to KILL the enemy pig King.

Important Tips:
1.You need to open the mod in endless and PVP mode. In the game, press Ctrl + F to attack hostile players and members. You can also set the peacetime.
2.Press 'Shift' key to sprint, press 'Y' key to group chat, press 'U' key to everyone chat.
3.Not to use it together with crypt. It may be not compatible with underground world. If the game crashes, please close other mods and try again, which may conflict with other mods.

---------------------------- Introduction ----------------------------

How to win
Get enough points,
or Kill the other teams' PigKing.

Tips
There are 4 teams you can fight for. You can also change this by going into MOD settings(language, random group, total score, and more).
Giving valuable items to your PigKing gives you points.
Killing other enemy players or boss monsters gives you points.
Buildings or crops set near the PigKing will be protected.
If your PigKing is killed, your team will be disbanded (You can also set PigKing invincibility).
In order to get the upper hand in the game, you need to protect your own PigKing.
There are some non-PVP days allowed so can improve your base and team.You can change this in MOD Setting Page.

Any advice welcome and don't forget to rate, Thanks.

Suggest starting game in endless-PVP mode and press Ctrl+F to attack Other team's players

It's about time, welcome to the DST PVP era... This is just the beginning!
